// Section One
Four Plausible Futures
Scenario Analysis for 2046-2056
// Scenario 1 — 15-25% Probability
Post-Anthropocene
Restoration & Harmony
The World: Climate stabilized below 2°C warming. Renewable energy dominance with fusion online. AI-augmented resource management. Localized economies with global coordination. Human lifespan extended to 120+ years.

Life in 2046: "Dynamic Living Spaces" — people can live anywhere. Work redefined as meaning over survival. Conscious-technology integration. Abundance-based economics emerging.

Required Path: Immediate global mobilization, 50% emissions reduction by 2035, complete energy transition by 2045.
Fusion Online Climate Stabilized Abundance Economics Lifespan 120+
// Scenario 2 — 30-40% Probability
Greentocracy
Managed Decline
The World: Climate impacts force emergency governance. Resource rationing via AI systems. Restricted mobility (carbon passports). Stratified societies: climate havens vs. sacrifice zones. Surveillance state for sustainability enforcement.

Life in 2046: Basic needs met through technology efficiency. Limited personal freedom, high security. Urban concentration continues. Healthcare advanced but access stratified. Virtual reality as escape from constrained physical world.

Triggers: Cascading climate disasters between 2030-2040 trigger emergency measures.
Emergency Gov Carbon Passports Stratified Access Surveillance State
// Scenario 3 — 25-35% Probability
Extinction Express
Collapse Trajectory
The World: 2-4°C warming crossed. Food/water/energy crises in cascading failure. Economic systems collapse. Nation-states fragment. Population decline to 6 billion by 2060. Mass migration and resource wars.

Life in 2046: Autonomous local communities. Subsistence agriculture returns. Urban depopulation to rural areas. Technology access fragmented. Barter and local currency economies. "Salvage civilization" — repurposing old tech.

Triggers: Tipping points crossed: ice sheet collapse, permafrost methane, ocean circulation shutdown.
Systemic Collapse Cascading Failures Local Resilience Salvage Economy
// Scenario 4 — 15-25% Probability
Humans Inc.
Techno-Feudalism
The World: Corporate states replace nation-states. AI systems optimize for efficiency over human values. Human labor largely obsolete. Basic income / "useless class" management. Biological/digital divide (enhanced vs. natural humans).

Life in 2046: Post-work society for most. AI companions, virtual existence. Stratified by access to enhancement technology. Meaning crisis, purpose through consumption/entertainment. Physical world degraded, digital world rich.

Path: Continued corporate consolidation + AGI breakthrough + geoengineering.
Corporate States Human Obsolescence Bio-Digital Divide Meaning Crisis
